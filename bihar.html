8. Annual Festivals Sonepur Mela, Sonepur:

Location: Sonepur, near Patna. Origin Story: Sonepur Mela, one of Asia's largest cattle fairs, dates back to ancient times. It originally served as a platform for trading elephants, horses, and other animals, and has evolved into a grand cultural event. The fair is held on Kartik Poornima, the full moon day, in the months of November and December. Uniqueness: The festival features a vibrant blend of trade, entertainment, and spirituality. It showcases Bihar's rural life with various cultural performances, folk art, and a diverse range of stalls. Price Range: Entry fees may apply; varies for specific events.

Where to Buy: Tickets available at the entrance.
