2. Local Crafts Ilkal Sarees: P K Guled Sarees:

Location: Ilkal town in Bagalkot district.
Ilkal Sarees Manufacturers and exporters 3809, New Kothwal Peth, Ward No-5 Taluk-Hungund District-Bagalkote ILKAL-587 125 KARNATAKA, INDIA
Origin Story: Ilkal sarees have a rich history dating back to the Chalukyan era.
Traditionally woven by the Devangas, a community of weavers, these sarees have a distinctive design and color palette.
Uniqueness: Known for their unique pallus and the use of khun fabric, Ilkal sarees showcase the vibrant cultural identity of Karnataka.
The sarees often feature peacock and temple designs.

5. Hidden Gems Jog Falls, Shimoga:

Location: Jog Falls is in the Shimoga district.
Origin Story: Formed by the Sharavathi River, Jog Falls is one of the highest plunge waterfalls in India.
It is a natural wonder surrounded by lush greenery.
Uniqueness: The falls are captivating during the monsoon season when the water volume is at its peak.
The misty atmosphere and the roaring sound of the falls create a mesmerizing experience.
Price Range: Entry fees vary; usually ₹5 ₹10 (approx.)

Where to Buy: Tickets available at the entrance.

8. Annual Festivals Hampi Festival, Hampi:

Location: The Hampi Festival is celebrated in the historic town of Hampi.
Origin Story: The festival started to promote the rich cultural heritage of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
It showcases traditional music, dance, and art forms of Karnataka.
Uniqueness: Hampi Festival brings the ruins of the Vijayanagar Empire to life with a vibrant cultural extravaganza.
It's a unique blend of history, art, and celebration.
Price Range: Entry to most events is free; some may have nominal fees.
